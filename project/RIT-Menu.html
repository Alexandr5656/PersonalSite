Rit-Menu

During my first year at my university as a final project we were asked to work as a group to come up with a application that would help students lives. Our school is known for having way too many webpages complicating the search for events or food around campus. The idea formed into RIT-Menu's where we would condense all of RIT's pages into a single point of access for the user. We also decided to add a calandar feature into the app as well to add another souce of organization to the app. This was fully written in C# using the .net framework.

My part of the project was to create the dining portion. I started out by creating a system that would dynamically display the hours of all the resturants on campus. Once set up I added the ability to filter out all the resturants depending on preferences and times. Afterwards I created a system to scrape RIT's dining websites to look for daily specials at each place so that users could find days where their favorite foods were being served. I scraped two weeks worth a data at a time and had it automatically saved to the users client everytime RIT updated their menus.
